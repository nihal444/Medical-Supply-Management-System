ABOUT Pharmacy Management System

While visiting a medical store, I encountered a situation where the required medicines were unavailable, and the shopkeeper mentioned having to go to the pharmacy to restock. This inspired the idea of introducing a management system that could supply products directly to medical shops, eliminating the need for them to go to the pharmacy. Subsequently, we implemented this concept in our database management system (DBMS) mini-project.
